TOBIASLUNGHI

By Tobias Lunghi on Aug 15, 2014


Genre - Comedy Vlogs


"TobiasLunghi" is a self-titled online comedy project, used as an outlet for my own comedic writing through the medium of sketches and vlogs. The channel presents a satire (and usually dark) tone of writing throughout most of the content featured, whether it's a touchy spoof tutorial on "How To Cook a Baby" or a anti-climatic thriller featuring young boys on a sofa with "Super Powers", it certainly strays from the conventionally handsome British YouTuber channel.

Created in the year 2012 at the age of 15, I started uploading sketch comedy videos with friends of mine who would play their own personalities, since most of my inspiration of sketches flare off conversations we share. Sometimes desperately trying to be funny and failing miserably.

The channel is considered a comedy project due to my distaste in the content that is being published currently by YouTubers my age. Many YouTubers feel like they have to cap their creativity to accommodate to their audience and limit their personalities. To some this means if they say or do anything objectable, their audience will criticize them in the comments. I strongly believe your content should always be first, rather than having to compromise material to please the internet. Hence the unhealthy doses of dark humor and profanity I use consistently and oh-so dearly love. Not only has publishing such material allowed me to push the boundaries of what's acceptable to post online, it also means that my future videos can never be predictable. Which is exactly how I imagined to the channel to be before I created the project, the minority and the unique.
