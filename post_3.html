BADLUCKBLUE

By Tom on Aug 24, 2014


Genre - Let's Play


Hi I'm Blue or BadLuckBlue if you prefer full names and I play a lot of indie games with the occasional big title game. I also shall be playing many games for next gen consoles in future and also progressing into some musical parodies and vlogging about daily life!

Throughout my life I have always had a deep relationship with games and the gaming world (nothing weird though, don’t worry I know it sounds bad!) and whenever I used to play games I would always give a full running commentary and my opinions on what was happening in said game until one day I thought “Hang on I should record this!” and so my channel was born! My name in itself started after a reputation I developed of always dying first and in horrendous ways! The prime example of this happened whilst playing Battlefield Bad Company Two online with my friends; we were traveling inside a helicopter across the map when we suddenly got hit by a rocket! I quickly jumped out of the helicopter thinking I had escaped only to have a passing jet fighter fly through my body and liquidise me! And so the name came to be.

As for the second part of my name I was originally part of a collective YouTube channel of friends called the ClanOfHippos that consisted of eight members each with a different colour mine of course as you can now see was Blue and so the inspiration for the second part of my name was born!
