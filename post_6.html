ICYGREY

By Jake on Aug 24, 2014


Genre - Commentaries


I’m a 19 year old fulltime Student at Aberystwyth University in Wales, studying Computer Science. I have been active on YouTube for a number of years now under different channels and finally found one that I feel confident sticking with!

It’s been about 10 months now to nearly a year on this channel and I’m still going strong! Having recently reached 250 subscribers and over 7000 upload views! My videos vary in content from Reviews, Gameplay Commentaries (for a variety of games) and Collection Updates / Gaming Pickups, with the hopes to start Let’s Plays soon.

I love interacting with my subscribers whether that be through YouTube, Facebook, Twitter or Xbox Live and I also spend my free time gaming with them on Xbox. I also enjoy helping out other YouTubers and collaborating, so I spend a lot of my time watching and giving feedback to other creators, as well as being active on the Freedom! forums. My YouTube channel is partnered with Freedom! through Etatch.
