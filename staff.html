CAREERS AT ETATCH

At Etatch we recognize that hard working, motivated individuals our most valuable asset. Whichever career path you chose within Etatch from recruiter to content writer, we expect a very professional standard, it is for this reason we provide an in depth training process getting you familiar with how the company operates. If you wish to find out more about what vacancies we have please email support@etatch.co.uk. Make sure to follow us on twitter - www.twitter.com/etatch - where we always publicise any vacancies. Upon applying for a particular role please provide a detailed message outlining any skills or experience you have that would benefit you in the job.



CURRENT VACANCIES


Position type: Content Scout

What the job entails: As an Etatch content scout, your role within the company is to search for respected YouTube channels, who have found some success, but could find even more with the right support. You will then be expected to outreach those channels via email. If they respond and are interested, you have to sell them the Network and ensure they sign with us.

All staff will be required to;

1. Bring in 100,000 new monthly views every month. What does this mean? When a channel signs using your referral link, you will need to check their average monthly views. If, for example, they average 23,000 monthly views, your quota will remain at 77,000. This quota also means Etatch staff are rewarded financially.

2. Work for 7+ hours every week. Why 7 hours? We feel an hour a day is a reasonable amount of time to be putting in to Etatch. This can be an hour a day, or 7 hours one day a week.

We would like all applicants to have one of the following; - Experience with YouTube networks, - Run their own YouTube channel - Sales experience

Training process: We provide an in depth training process getting you up to speed with the Job. This includes company knowledge, sales techniques and getting used to the software we use.

Payment: The role is commission based (this will be discussed in more detail with anyone interested). There is also a great bonus scheme in place! Meaning if you have a bad few weeks, you don't miss out!
